REMODELING

When a home functions well, but no longer brings joy, it is time for an interior facelift. After discussions with the homeowner, Studio Boise coined a style "mid century glam," an aesthetic rooted in minimalism but embellished with color and patterns more typical of maximalism. Pairings of brass and blush, forest green and gray scallops, glass globes, and art deco patterns were carefully combined to create an ambiance that brings a youthful joy to an old home and acts as an extension of the homeowner's personality.

MID CENTURY KITCHEN

While the aesthetic design of mid century modern homes has remained nearly unchanged in its appeal, authentic mid century homes fail in one unforgivable way: disconnected, small kitchens. Studio Boise designed this kitchen remodel with a small addition to provide a fitting mid century modern kitchen for the contemporary family.
